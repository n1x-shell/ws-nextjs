# TUNNELCORE — WORLD MAP (MACRO LAYOUT)

-----

## CITY OVERVIEW

The city has no name anyone agrees on. Helixion calls it a "cognitive infrastructure zone." BCI calls it Sector 7. The people who live in it call it what it is: a machine that eats people and calls it progress.

Helixion Dynamics sits at the center — a megastructure that rises above everything and houses BCI's Directorate 9 on its upper floors. Corporate and state power share the same spine. Everything else radiates outward from that core in clusters separated by dead zones, security corridors, and the slow decay of infrastructure that stops getting maintained the further you get from the money.

The city is vertical. Three traversal layers exist almost everywhere:

```
ROOFTOPS    Antenna arrays, mechanical spaces, signal pirate territory.
            A parallel highway above the streets. Accessible from most districts.

STREETS     Where the world pretends to function. Districts, commerce, surveillance.

UNDERCITY   Drainage tunnels, abandoned transit, hidden facilities.
            Mirrors the surface near the top. Gets weird deeper down.
            Everything converges at the Substrate.
```

The Freemarket isn't a place — it's a network. Vendor stalls, fences, food carts, and information brokers threaded through every district. The Freemarket faction has eyes everywhere because they ARE everywhere.

## ZONE DIRECTORY

### SURFACE ZONES (7)

| # | Zone | Location | Size | Key Features |
|---|------|----------|------|-------------|
| 1 | **Helixion Campus** | Center | 12-15 rooms | Corporate megastructure. Labs, offices, security checkpoints. BCI/Directorate 9 on upper floors. Highest security in the game. Late-game access. |
| 2 | **Residential Blocks** | East | 12-15 rooms | Dense housing towers. Mesh-compliant civilians. Surveilled. Quiet desperation. Helixion clinics, compliance centers. Drainage grates in alleys. |
| 3 | **Industrial District** | Southeast, waterfront | 15-18 rooms | Factories, chop shops, warehouses. Chrome Wolves territory. Chemical runoff feeds drainage below. Waterfront edge. |
| 4 | **The Fringe (Ruins)** | West | 10-12 rooms | Collapsed old city. Abandoned buildings. Scavengers, feral augments. Drainage Nexus access below. Iron Bloom surface entrance at southern border. |
| 5 | **The Fringe (Nomads)** | Far East | 8-10 rooms | Beyond the residential perimeter. Nomad camps, open ground, escape routes. Off-grid living. Signal relay territory. |
| 6 | **Fight Pits** | Industrial/Fringe border | 5-8 rooms | Chrome Wolves arena. PvP and PvE. Lawless zone — entering is consent. Chop shop, betting, gear trade. |
| 7 | **Rooftop Network** | Spans all districts | 10-15 rooms | Mechanical spaces, antenna arrays, catwalks. Signal pirate territory. Fast traversal. Accessible from most surface zones via fire escapes, ladders, maintenance hatches. |

### UNDERCITY ZONES (6)

| # | Zone | Depth | Location (relative to surface) | Size | Key Features |
|---|------|-------|-------------------------------|------|-------------|
| 8 | **Drainage Nexus** | Shallow | Beneath Fringe (ruins/west) | 12-15 rooms | Central tunnel hub. Parish territory. Origin point. Water, rust, chemical runoff. Community in wreckage. |
| 9 | **Maintenance Tunnels** | Shallow | Beneath Residential + Helixion | 10-12 rooms | Service corridors, cable runs. Surveilled sections near Helixion. Drainage grate connections to surface. |
| 10 | **Industrial Drainage** | Shallow | Beneath Industrial District | 10-12 rooms | Heavy runoff channels. Chrome Wolves stash rooms. Chemical hazards. Connects to deeper transit. |
| 11 | **Abandoned Transit** | Deep | Fragmented, under multiple districts | 15-18 rooms | Broken metro lines. Flooded stations. Collapsed passages. Offset connections — shortcuts that don't match surface geography. Unpredictable. |
| 12 | **Iron Bloom Server Farm** | Deep | Beneath fringe/industrial border | 10-12 rooms | Resistance HQ. Augmentation clinic. Dr. Serrano's workshop. Connected to surface entrance above and transit network. |
| 13 | **Black Market Warrens** | Deep | Beneath multiple districts | 8-10 rooms | Underground commerce. No faction controls it. Weapons, cyberware, information. High prices, no questions. |

### ENDGAME ZONES (3)

| # | Zone | Depth | Size | Key Features |
|---|------|-------|------|-------------|
| 14 | **The Substrate Level** | Deepest | 10-15 rooms | Beneath Helixion. Only accessible from below (through drainage/transit). 33hz amplified. Neural tissue architecture. GHOST ≥ 7 for some rooms. The Signal faction. Lore endgame. |
| 15 | **Broadcast Tower** | All layers | 8-12 rooms | Rises above every rooftop, descends through all layers to the Substrate. Helixion's endgame weapon. Under construction until Act 4. Frequency capture infrastructure. Final battle location. |
| 16 | **Helixion Lab** | Instanced | 8-10 rooms | Raid-style dungeon within the Helixion Campus. Multi-room, multi-boss. Party recommended. Chrysalis research. Can be run multiple times. |

### TOTAL: ~180–210 rooms across 16 zones

### Fast Travel Nodes

| Type | Locations | Requirement |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| **Transit Station** | Residential, Industrial, Helixion perimeter (surface) | Credits. Connects surface districts. |
| **Drainage Access** | Most surface districts (down), most shallow tunnels (up) | None. Takes 1 turn. Can be ambushed. |
| **Signal Relay** | Rooftop Network, Drainage Nexus, Iron Bloom, Substrate Level | GHOST ≥ 6. Instant. Frequency hop on 33hz. |
| **Iron Bloom Shuttle** | Iron Bloom HQ to 2–3 allied locations | Quest-gated. Must complete Iron Bloom questline. |
