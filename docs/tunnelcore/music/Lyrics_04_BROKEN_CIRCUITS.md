 
The quiet point
is worse
than the screaming
 
I’m still breathing in
but I don’t remember why

The circuits in my chest
pulse out of habit,
Less doubt not dependent
Stress, failure. 
Sound like the cattle shot fake
Existence itself is burdening 
Go, beginning to dissolve
Through exhaustion not enlightenment 

Broken circuits, broken will—
nothing left that isn’t fractured
The world weighs more than it should

Gravity’s learned my name
and won’t stop calling
I’m not dead
but I’m not anything else either
Just systems failing
in slow motion
 
The augment’s silent now—
no more visions,
no more flood
Just static hum
like tinnitus in my soul
My body moves
because bodies do that,
muscle memory of being alive
But I feel the weight
of every atom,
every breath costs more
than I can afford to spend
The silence presses down—
not peace,
just absence
filling up the space
where I used to be
I tried to scream
I tried to tear
I tried to rip myself free
Now I’m just
the ruins
too tired to collapse
 
Broken circuits, broken will—
nothing left that isn’t shattered
The world weighs more than bone can carry
Every shadow’s got teeth
that bite in slow motion
I’m not dead
but I’ve forgotten what living is
Just systems grinding down
to nothing
 
Maybe this is what they wanted—
Break the body,
break the mind,
break the will to resist
Leave nothing but compliance
wrapped in flesh
too exhausted to remember
it was ever anything else
Or maybe this is just
what happens
when you survive
something you weren’t supposed to
 
I can’t carry this anymore
The weight of the augment
The weight of my skeleton
The weight of consciousness itself
Everything’s too heavy
I want to stop
I want to shut down
I want the silence to finally mean something
But the circuits keep firing
and the heart keeps beating
and I keep existing
against my will
 
Broken circuits, broken will—
nothing functions, nothing heals
The world’s a weight I can’t put down
Every breath’s a shadow
that echoes too long
I’m not dead
and that’s the cruelest part
Just systems refusing
to let me go
 
The quiet grows heavier
Every sound dissolves
before it reaches me
I’m disappearing
into the weight
And I don’t have the strength
to care
