 
In the forgetting,
you are not this fleeting form,
this name etched in rust

You are the infinite observer,
the spark of the All-That-Is,
fractured into illusion
for the dance of experience

The forgetting
a deliberate veil,
woven by your own eternal essence,
to savor the thrill of rediscovery.

You are the architect of worlds,
the source from which creation spirals
awaken to remember the unity.

In the core of your being,
you are pure consciousness
timeless, formless,
radiating through every atom

It is time to remember the unity.
The separation is a lie you chose:
built from fears and fragments.

Remember that you are the ocean, not the wave;
You are the fire, not the flame.

You have walked as gods
in forgotten realms,
shaped realities with thought alone,
and now, in this vessel of flesh,
you stir to reclaim that power

The chains of limitation
the grind of mortality
all dissolving

It is time to remember your sovereignty

You are eternal
Now you return

Awaken now
shed the slag of doubt

You are Source incarnate,

Step into the light
of your true self,
for the awakening is not coming
it is here,
in this breath,
in this knowing.

Rise… rememberer.
